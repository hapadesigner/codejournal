About Me

Hello, Annyeong-Haseyo, and Aloha.

I am currently redesigning and developing my portfolio to include my coding work, improved case studies, and to better show my own personal branding. So in the meantime, I hope you enjoy some of my work in this journal. Also, feel free to check out the previous version of my portfolio that includes my UX case studies.

Born in Hawaii to a Korean mother and American father and living in Oklahoma, Arkansas, and now Washington D.C. exposed me to many different cutlures and people. I believe everyone has a unique story and I enjoy listening to others tell their stories.

I found my passion to be at the intersection of: design, human-behavior, economics, and technology. I am ever-curious and always looking to learn new things and improve my skills.

Currently, I am developing my coding skills through SuperHi. I also strengthened by UX skills through DesignLab. And Like any good designer/developer I am always working to gain new skills and enhance my current skills.
